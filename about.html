Welcome to our Foundation

The Le Shelter Foundation (LSF) is a humanitarian NGO founded in 2017 by Ruby Offeibea Adu. Dedicated to inclusivity, empathy, and community service, LSF focuses on improving the lives of underprivileged children in rural Ghana, aligning its mission with global development goals.

Through various initiatives, LSF provides essential school supplies like books and uniforms to bridge educational gaps. Additionally, it offers mentorship programs to guide young learners in career and personal development.
